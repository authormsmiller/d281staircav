  Late June 1971 — SE of FSB Fontaine, Binh Tuy Province. Davis's platoon (Range) 
  entered a large VC bunker complex and supply depot in a high saddle of a small

Kirk Davis served with Range platoon, D Co. 2/8 Cav, arriving in 1971. He remained 
an active member of the Angry Skipper Association through at least 2016, when he 
co-produced the Range 70-71 DVD with James "Hawk" Bryant. He also created the 
"Your Tour in Vietnam" commemorative print, offered to fellow veterans at cost. As 
of 2013 he was living in Lake Jackson, Texas.

Davis wrote at least two pieces for the *Walking Point* newsletter: "30 Minutes 
Later" (Issue 18, January 2013), a firsthand account of the Binh Tuy bunker complex 
raid, and "Getting Old" (Issue 25, January 2017).
